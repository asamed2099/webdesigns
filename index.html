Baskin Africa

Founded in 2018, BASKIN AFRICA is a Public Policy Think Tank with the overall goal of ensuring that the critical resources and sectors of the Ghanaian economy in particular but Africa at large, are carefully harnessed and expended to the benefit of citizens, in an inclusive, equitable, accountable and sustainable manner. read more

Mission

To expose deficient public and corporate actions, programmes and policies, while generating corresponding knowledge and alternatives, to advance accountability, inclusivity, equity and sustainable development.

Vision

To become the center of excellence in Africa where contemporary relevant knowledge, capacities and policy alternatives are generated for sustainable, inclusive and equitable development.

Core Values

We are committed to:

  • Inclusiveness
  • Integrity
  • Equity
  • Accountability
  • Excellence

Issifu Seidu kudus Gbeadese

Executive Secretary

The Executive Secretary is a Ghanaian, born and raised in Damongo-Savannah Regional Capital. He’s a native of Laribanga-a Kamara by tribe. He had his basic and secondary education in Damongo. He’s an Economic Policy Analyst and a Research Assistant in the office of Parliament. The Executive Secretary has Diploma in Basic Education from the Nusrat Jahan College of Education, Wa; a Bachelor’s Degree in Political Science from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Kumasi; a Master’s of Art Degree in Economic Policy Management from the University of Ghana and currently reading LLB program in the Wisconsin International University College. The Executive Secretary is a writer: a blogger and a columnist with Modernghana.com. The column name is: https://www.modernghana.com/author/IssifuSeiduKudusGbeadese. He has published about 70 articles in 2 years on his column. Aside being a volunteer and a community development advocate for several years, Mr Issifu Seidu Kudus Gbeadese is a lead advocate of Settle Ghana’s Free, Prior and Informed Consent (FPIC), aimed at establishing a bottom-up participation and consultation of the indigenous people of Ghana prior to the beginning of exploitationof the natural resources of any kind. Prior to his appointment as an Executive Secretary of Baskin Africa, Mr. Issifu Seidu Kudus Gbeadese has been the Public Relations Officer of the organization since July 2018.
